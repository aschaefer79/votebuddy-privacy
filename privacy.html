Privacy Policy for VoteBuddy

Last updated: 2026

VoteBuddy is a mobile application designed to conduct local votes on a single device. The app operates completely offline and does not require user accounts or internet connectivity.

Data Collection

VoteBuddy does not collect, transmit, or share personal data with the developer or any third parties.

Local Storage

All voting data entered into the app is stored locally on the user's device.

Data Transmission

The app does not transmit data to external servers and does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Import and Export

The app allows exporting and importing voting data as files (e.g., JSON or CSV). Users are responsible for how they store or share these files.
